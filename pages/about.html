  • Hey I'm Matthew Ma!

    I am a UX designer who is currently pursuing my studies at Simon Fraser University (SFU) in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology. My passion for design started at a young age when I was fascinated with how products and services could be improved through thoughtful design. Born and raised in Vancouver, Canada, I had always been interested in technology and the way it impacts people's lives. After completing high school, I decided to pursue my passion for design by enrolling in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at SFU. During my time at SFU, I have honed my design skills by taking courses in user-centered design, prototyping, and user research. I have also been involved in various design projects, including designing a mobile app for a local business and creating a user-friendly website for a non-profit organization. I am passionate about creating designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functional and intuitive. I believe that design should be accessible to everyone and I work hard to ensure that my designs are inclusive and user-friendly. Outside of my studies, I enjoy attending design conferences and workshops to stay up-to-date with the latest design trends and techniques. I am also an active member of the UX design community in Vancouver and enjoy collaborating with other designers on various projects. After graduating from SFU, I plan to pursue a career in UX design, where I hopes to continue creating designs that improve people's lives.
