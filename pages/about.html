  • Hey I'm Matthew Ma!

    I am a UX designer who is currently pursuing my studies at Simon Fraser University (SFU) in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology. My passion for design started at a young age when I was fascinated with how products and services could be improved through thoughtful design.

    Born and raised in Vancouver, Canada, I had always been interested in technology and the way it impacts people's lives. After completing high school, I decided to pursue my passion for design by enrolling in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at SFU.

    I have a deep understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and I leverage these skills to bring my designs to life. Outside of my studies, I enjoy attending design conferences and workshops to stay up-to-date with the latest design trends and techniques. I am also an active member of the UX design community in Vancouver and enjoy collaborating with other designers on various projects.

    After graduating from SFU, I plan to pursue a career in UX design, where I hopes to continue creating designs that improve people's lives.
